searchable listboxes, menu/navigation composites, date grids, and carousel
slide regions.

## Conclusion

Stage 4 is complete. Each Stage 4 widget or disposition has source or an
explicit recipe/block outcome, shared reference/candidate scenarios, artifact
coverage, focused behavior tests, documentation, and reviewed divergence notes.

Issue 2 can move to Stage 5 next: `chart`, `data-table`, `sonner`, `toast`,
`resizable`, `sidebar`, `form`, `input-group`, `input-otp`, and any remaining
React-heavy system or block dispositions.
